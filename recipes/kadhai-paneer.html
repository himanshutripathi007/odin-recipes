Kadhai Paneer

Description

Kadhai Paneer is a spicy and aromatic North Indian dish made with paneer, capsicum, tomatoes, and special kadhai masala. It is perfect with roti or naan.

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes
  • Capsicum (sliced)
  • Tomatoes (pureed)
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Kadhai masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Coriander leaf
  • Garam masala
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil and saut√© onions and capsicum.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  3. Mix in tomato puree and spices, then cook until thick.
  4. Add paneer cubes and kadhai masala.
  5. Cook for 5 minutes and Spread coriander leaf then serve hot with roti or naan.
