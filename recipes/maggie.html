Maggie

Description

Indian-style Maggi is a spicy and flavorful twist on the classic instant noodles. It includes onions, tomatoes, and Indian spices, making it a quick and delicious comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Oil
  • Maggi tastemaker
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions and chilies.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Mix in turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala.
  4. Add water and bring it to a boil.
  5. Add noodles and tastemaker, then cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
